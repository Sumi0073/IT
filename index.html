VISION:

To be recognized for technological advancement of the Department in teaching, learning, research and innovation and serve to address evolving global needs.

  • Promote interdisciplinary research and innovation in emerging areas of Information Technology.
  • To prepare learners with leadership qualities, team-spirit and ethical responsibilities with a positive application of knowledge to the society
